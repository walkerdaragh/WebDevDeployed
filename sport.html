Football

There are four football teams in our area for all age groups - young children to senior adults. Spanning across many levels, there is a team for everyone. Our football season begins in February and ends in November, finishing up for the Winter break. If you would like to get involved please contact football@walnutbay.com


GAA

GAA is Ireland's traditional sport, with the first game of Gaelic Football under GAA rules first played way back in 1885. With Gaelic Football, Hurling and Camogie, there is an Irish sport available for everyone. There are three Gaelic Football teams in Walnut Bay - two which cater for all ages, while we have one dedicated Senior Team.


Athletics

In Walnut Bay, we have a very popular Athletics Club - Walnut Harriers. Walnut Harriers are very active, with a running club who meet up three times a week, an athletics division, and their very own 800 meter running track. Walnut Harriers also participate in 5k, 10, half-marathon, and marathon races throughout the year.
